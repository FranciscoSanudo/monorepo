About Francisco Sanudo

Greetings, I’m Francisco Sanudo, an engineer who transitioned from the world of chemical engineering into the realm of technology. My diverse journey began with significant contributions to advanced industrial control systems in the manufacturing industry. I then embarked on a path in technology, diving into programming with Python and Linux.

As a self-motivated, self-taught practitioner, I’ve had the freedom to select the right tools for specific problems, allowing me to explore various technology stacks.

My current focus includes:

AI Enthusiast: I’m deeply interested in AI and currently experimenting with AI tools, striving to understand their unique strengths and applications.

Data Maven: I harness the power of data through engineering and visualization to drive insightful decisions.

SRE & DevOps Advocate: Ensuring smooth system operations is a priority, with a focus on Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) and DevOps.

Language Explorer: While Python remains my primary choice, I’m currently immersing myself in the world of Lisp, exploring its potential in AI and technology.

This ongoing journey of exploration and learning fuels my enthusiasm, and I look forward to sharing my insights and discoveries on this website. Feel free to connect or follow my journey for updates.
